Alexandra Hoefinger

Hi! I'm a web developer and handmade crafter living in Philly.

A former English teacher in NYC, I recently finished a web development apprenticeship at P'unk Avenue, where I gained experience in building sites with HTML, CSS, and client/server-side JavaScript. Currently I work on the front end dev team at Urban Outfitters, maintaining the site's desktop and mobile homepages and nav menus. To sharpen my skills, I regularly participate in local tech meetups and attend a range of GirlDevelopIt classes as a student and TA.

When I'm not working on tech-related things, I make and sell reclaimed wood furniture, rustic jewelry, knitwear, candles, and lots of other handmade creations for my Etsy shop, Philodendron.

Formerly an English teacher in NYC, I transitioned to a career in programming out of an interest in creating and building things on the web. Now as a front-end developer at Urban Outfitters in Philadelphia, I work with JavaScript — mainly AngularJS and ES6 these days — to maintain and build new features on urbanoutfitters.com.

In addition to leading workshops at a number of local tech events, I'm also actively involved in GDI Philly as an instructor and volunteer. When I'm not spending my time hacking on web projects, I work on lots of handmade craft projects and blog about them at philodendron.co.
